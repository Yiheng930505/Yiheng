Brief Biography 

Mr. Yi-Heng Zhu received his B.S. degree in computer science from Nanjing Institute of Technology in 2015. From 2015, he acted as a Ph.D Student of School of Computer Science and Engineering in NanJing University of Science and Technology and a member of Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group, led by professor Dong-Jun Yu. From 2019 to 2021, he acted as a visiting student at the University of Michigan (Ann Arbor) in U.S.A, funded by China Scholarship Council and led by professor Yang Zhang. He is interested in the following topics:

  • Protein Function (Gene Ontology) Prediction

  • Protein-Ligand Binding Sites Prediction

  • Protein Crystallization Prediction

  • Protein-Protein Interaction Network

  • Deep Learning in Bioinformatics
